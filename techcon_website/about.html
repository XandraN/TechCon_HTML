Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 by a group of technology enthusiasts who wanted to create a platform for sharing cutting-edge ideas. What started as a small gathering of 200 attendees has now grown into one of the world's premier tech conferences, hosting over 10,000 participants annually.

Over the years, TechCon has been hosted in various tech hubs around the world, including San Francisco, Berlin, Tokyo, and now returning to our original home in Silicon Valley for our 15th anniversary edition.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to bridge the gap between technology and humanity by fostering innovation, collaboration, and knowledge sharing. We believe technology should be accessible, inclusive, and beneficial to all.

Each year, we strive to:

  • Showcase groundbreaking technological advancements
  • Provide a platform for underrepresented voices in tech
  • Facilitate meaningful connections between attendees
  • Inspire the next generation of tech leaders
